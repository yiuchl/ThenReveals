Which Then Reveals Another emerged in September 2023 as an exploration of “Beauty is truth, truth beauty,” which is line 49 of a poem John Keats wrote in 1819.


Clearly, I had a lot to think about.


While walking in the city, I liked how light filters and reflects. revealing soft, shifting shadows, demonstrating how perception constantly evolves.


The leaves themselves are leaves, and their shadows, too, are leaves. Multiple perspectives converge to form a complex .


From there, I made printed and digital matter representing truth and beauty.


And here we are.
